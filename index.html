About me

My name is Jinshuo Dong. I'm a PhD student in applied math (AMCS) at the University of Pennsylvania, with the great fortune to be advised by Aaron Roth. Currently the main focus of my work is on differential privacy and its role in statistics and machine learning. I'm also generally interested in expanding my (hopefully in the future non-)negligible knowledge in the mathematics of information and computation.

Feel free to write me at firstname@sas.upenn.edu. For publication and more information, see my homepage.
